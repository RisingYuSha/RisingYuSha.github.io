I am Yu Sha (🦈沙毓🦈), now a Postdoctoral Researcher / Assistant Researcher at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen (CUHK-Shenzhen). During 2019 to 2025, I was a PhD student at Xidian University, co-advised by Prof. Bo Liu and Prof. Shuiping Gou. During 2020 to 2022, I was a visiting scientist in "Deepthinkers" group at Frankfurt Institute for Advanced Studies (FIAS), co-advised by Prof. Horst Stöcker and Prof. Kai Zhou. In addition, during 2020 to 2025, I am a member of Xidian-FIAS Joint Research Center (XFJRC) and Giersch Science Center (GSC). Before that, I received the B.Sc degree from Lanzhou University of Technology in 2019.

My research interests include AI for Industry/Science (especially AI for acoustic damage detection and diagnosis) and Physics-Informed Machine Learning / Deep Learning. I have published 11+ papers () in top international journals and conferences, such as SIGKDD, EAAI, ESWA.
